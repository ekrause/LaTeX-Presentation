The three types of lists:\\\\
Itemize:
\begin{itemize}
  \item these are bulleted
  \item as such
\end{itemize}
Enumerate:
\begin{enumerate}
  \item these are numbered
  \item as you might expect
\end{enumerate}
Description:
\begin{description}
  \item[thing A] text 
  \item[thing B] words 
\end{description}
